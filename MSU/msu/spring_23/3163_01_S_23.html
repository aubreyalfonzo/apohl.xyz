JUMPING AHEAD / BAUHAUS

The Bauhaus movement is one of the most prominent and prolific design movements to ever take place in history, despite its relatively short life.

The Bauhaus movement is significant for a multitude of reasons, and was revolutionary not only in the treatment of form and function in design, but also politically and culturally.
