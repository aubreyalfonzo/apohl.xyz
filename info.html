CV

  Aubrey Pohl (he/him) is a designer, professor, printmaker, and design researcher. Accessibility of design and material, social awareness, exposure of structure and process, data abstraction, ephemera, change, and research-informed, goal-oriented design are at the center of his practice (among other things).
•
Aubrey currently works as an Assistant Professor of Graphic Design at the Mississippi State University College of Art, Architecture, and Design.
•
Research focuses include:
Experimental methods of data visualization and data abstraction.
Alternative (accesible, affordable) methods for design, image making, and production.
Vexillology (flag design).

*These areas of research often overlap.
*These areas of research are in a constant state of flux.
